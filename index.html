The Studio

Quality Agency LATAM was founded in Rio de Janeiro by a collective of senior designers who, after more than a decade of independent practice across Brazil's most demanding markets, chose to unite under a single name — and look outward, toward Europe and beyond.

We are not a studio of generalists learning on the job. Every designer at QUAL holds postgraduate or master's-level training, and our combined portfolio spans over ten years of senior-level work across branding, packaging, and structural design. Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you work with contracts?

Yes. Every project begins with a signed contract outlining scope, timeline and payment terms, so both sides are protected and aligned from day one.

Do you issue invoices?

Yes, we issue a formal invoice (nota fiscal) for every project.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments via PayPal and Wise, making it simple to pay in Euros, Pounds, Dollars or other major currencies.

How does your timeline work?

We work with agility. Your exact delivery timeline is defined right after our project briefing call, and the clock starts once the initial 50% payment is received.

How does payment and delivery work, step by step?

50% of the agreed value is paid upfront to begin the project — this is when the timeline starts. We then deliver preview images (JPEG) for your approval. If changes are needed, we revise until you approve the final visuals. Once approved, the remaining 50% is due before we release the original, production-ready files.

Can you handle international packaging regulations?

Yes — our team has working familiarity with EU, UK and international packaging and labeling regulatory requirements, and we factor this into every structural and label design.
